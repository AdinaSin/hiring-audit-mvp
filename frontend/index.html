The Framework

7 dimensions of hiring health

Each block examines a critical layer of your hiring system. Cross-validation between blocks reveals what single perspectives hide.

1

Executive Ownership

Is hiring owned at the business level or delegated without accountability?

2

TA Leadership

Can your talent acquisition team execute with capacity, SLAs, and process discipline?

3

Delivery & Hiring

Do delivery teams enable or obstruct hiring through capacity and feedback?

4

Financial Governance

Is hiring spend visible, planned, and tied to business outcomes?

5

Technical Interviewing

Is evaluation structured, accountable, and operating within SLAs?

6

Recruitment Operations

Does recruitment run as a stable system or depend on heroics?

7

Reporting & AI

Is your data trustworthy and is AI governed or running wild?
