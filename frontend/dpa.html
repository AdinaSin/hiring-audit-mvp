Data Processing Agreement

Last updated: February 2025 · GDPR Article 28 Compliant

This DPA applies automatically when you use HiringAudit services. By purchasing and using the service, you accept this Data Processing Agreement.

1. Parties

  • Data Controller: You (the Client purchasing the audit)
  • Data Processor: HiringAudit (us)

2. Definitions

  • GDPR: General Data Protection Regulation (EU) 2016/679
  • Personal Data: Any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person
  • Processing: Any operation performed on Personal Data
  • Sub-processor: Third party engaged by us to process Personal Data
  • Data Subject: The individual whose Personal Data is processed

3. Subject Matter and Details

Purpose: Provision of hiring audit diagnostic services

Duration: Duration of service agreement plus 90 days

Data Subjects: Client employees, contractors, and representatives

Data Types: Name, email, job title, questionnaire responses, IP address, timestamps

4. Processor Obligations

As your Data Processor, we will:

  1. Process Personal Data only on your documented instructions
  2. Ensure personnel are bound by confidentiality obligations
  3. Implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures
  4. Obtain your authorization before engaging Sub-processors
  5. Assist you in responding to Data Subject requests
  6. Assist you in meeting GDPR compliance obligations
  7. Delete or return all Personal Data upon termination
  8. Make available information to demonstrate compliance

5. Security Measures

Technical Measures

  • TLS 1.2+ encryption for data in transit
  • AES-256 encryption for data at rest
  • Multi-factor authentication for system access
  • Regular security patching
  • Intrusion detection systems
  • Automated backups with encryption

Organizational Measures

  • Role-based access control (RBAC)
  • Employee security training
  • Confidentiality agreements
  • Security policies and procedures
  • Incident response plan

6. Sub-processors

You provide general authorization for us to engage Sub-processors. Current Sub-processors:

Sub-processor Purpose Location Safeguards
Vercel Website hosting USA / Global SCCs / DPF
Stripe Payment processing USA DPF Certified
Google Analytics Website analytics USA SCCs / DPF

We will notify you of any changes to Sub-processors with 30 days notice.

7. International Transfers

When transferring Personal Data outside the EEA, we rely on:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
  • Adequacy decisions by the European Commission
  • Data Privacy Framework (DPF) certification

8. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a Personal Data breach, we will:

  • Notify you within 48 hours of becoming aware
  • Provide details including: nature of breach, categories of data affected, approximate number of data subjects, likely consequences, and measures taken

9. Data Subject Rights

We will assist you in fulfilling Data Subject requests for:

  • Access to their Personal Data
  • Rectification of inaccurate data
  • Erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction of processing
  • Data portability
  • Objection to processing

10. Audit Rights

You have the right to audit our compliance with this DPA. Audits will be conducted with reasonable notice and during normal business hours.

11. Termination

Upon termination of services, we will:

  • Delete all Personal Data within 30 days, or
  • Return all Personal Data to you upon request
  • Provide written certification of deletion

12. Liability

Liability under this DPA is subject to the limitations in our Terms of Service, except for breaches of GDPR obligations which shall be governed by applicable law.
